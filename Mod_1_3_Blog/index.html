This is where it all began. I was working as a hotdog man in the mall and I slipped on a hamburger bun

I was in so much pain, I didn't know what to do with my life! I couldn't get out of bed or into bed. I feel like I was stuck in some sort of crouching position limbo...



And that's when it came to me! This is crouching position is pretty much a sit up. If I just get in and out of bed 100 times a day, it must work something out! Right! I trained harder than I have ever trained in my life. And now I feel better than I did as a hotdog mascot in the mall! With these simple tips and tricks I want to help you get winter ready!

So, I started my own Cross-Fit company "Get Out of Bed". It's simple, it incorporates getting in and out of bed, getting off the couch and sitting on the couch. These are the daily motions that we make that I knew had to be capitalized on. So do just sit there and be a bum, sit there and feel the burn! Contact me at
